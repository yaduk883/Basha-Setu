Our Mission

Why India needs better
language AI

India has 22 constitutionally recognized languages and hundreds of dialects — yet most AI models perform well only in English. The gap is not due to lack of intelligence, but lack of data.

BhashaSetu was created to close this gap. By collecting carefully verified, parallel translation data from human contributors, we're building the foundation for AI that truly understands and speaks the languages of 1.4 billion people.

Every sentence you translate brings us one step closer to AI that works for everyone — not just English speakers.

How It Works

Four simple steps to
quality training data

We've designed the entire workflow to be fast, guided, and error-resistant — so contributors can focus on language, not tools.

1

Choose a language pair

Select source and target language from our 12+ supported Indian languages plus English.

2

Type or paste your translation

Enter text in both columns. The app validates that each column is in the correct script automatically.

3

Auto-clean & save

We strip emojis, extra spaces, and zero-width characters before saving to Supabase.

4

Review & approve

Team members review entries in the approval queue before they're marked as training-ready.

Features

Everything you need to
collect quality data

Unicode script validation

Automatically detects if you're typing in the wrong language or script, and blocks saves with a clear error message.

Auto text cleaning

Strips emojis, collapses extra whitespace, removes zero-width characters, and shows you a live cleaned preview before saving.

Duplicate detection

Checks if the same source text already exists for the selected language pair before allowing a save, keeping your dataset clean.

Review & approval queue

Every entry goes through a human review step. Reviewers can approve or reject entries, ensuring only quality data is marked as training-ready.

Live dashboard & charts

Track entries per language pair, approval rates, and data distribution with interactive charts — filterable by language.

Filtered CSV export

Download your data as CSV with active filters applied — export only Approved entries, or a specific language pair, with one click.

Dark mode

Full dark mode toggle with your preference saved between sessions — easy on the eyes during long contribution sessions.

Unsaved data warning

If you try to close the tab with unsaved text in the editor, the browser will warn you before losing your work.

Pagination

The data table paginates at 50 rows — so the app stays fast and responsive even with thousands of entries in the database.

About Us

Built by language
enthusiasts, for AI

BhashaSetu (meaning "language bridge" in Sanskrit) was started with a simple belief: AI should work for everyone, in every language.

We are a small, passionate team of NLP researchers, linguists, and engineers who saw firsthand how underrepresented Indian languages are in modern AI systems — and decided to do something about it.

Our approach is human-first. We believe the quality of a translation dataset is only as good as the human knowledge behind it. Every entry in BhashaSetu goes through a human review before it becomes part of any model's training data.

We welcome contributors of all backgrounds — native speakers, language students, and bilingual professionals. Every sentence counts.

FAQ

Questions & answers

Do I need to be a professional translator?
No. We welcome anyone with strong bilingual proficiency. Native speakers, bilingual students, teachers, and professionals are all valuable contributors. The review queue is there to ensure quality regardless of your background.
What happens to the data I contribute?
All data is stored in our Supabase database and used exclusively for training open NLP models for Indian languages. We do not sell data or share it with third parties. Contributors remain anonymous by default.
Why does the app block my save sometimes?
The app uses Unicode script detection to verify that you're typing in the correct language for each column. If it detects that more than 70% of your text isn't in the expected script, it will block the save and tell you why. This keeps the dataset clean and reliable.
Can I export the data?
Yes. Admin team members can export data as a CSV file. The export respects active filters — you can export only Approved entries or a specific language pair. Admin access is required.
How do I get access to the app?
The app is accessible directly from this page — click "Open App" to start contributing translations. Admin features (Browse Data, Review Queue, Export CSV) require a password available to team members only.
